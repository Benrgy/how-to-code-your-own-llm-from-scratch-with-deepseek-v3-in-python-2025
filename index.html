Frequently Asked Questions

What technical prerequisites are required?

You should have intermediate Python programming skills and basic understanding of machine learning concepts. Familiarity with PyTorch is helpful but not required - we'll cover everything you need to know.

How much time should I dedicate to complete the course?

Most students complete the course in 8-10 weeks, dedicating 10-15 hours per week. The course is self-paced, so you can adjust the schedule to fit your needs.

What support is available if I get stuck?

You'll have access to our community forum, weekly office hours with AI experts, and detailed documentation. We also provide 1-on-1 technical support for complex issues.

Will I be able to build production-ready models?

Yes! The course covers everything from basic architecture to production deployment, including optimization techniques used in industry. You'll build multiple production-quality models throughout the course.

What hardware requirements are needed?

While a GPU is recommended for faster training, all exercises can be completed using Google Colab's free tier. We provide setup guides for both local and cloud-based development environments.
